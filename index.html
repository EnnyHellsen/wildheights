ABOUT WILD HEIGHTS

The Wild Heights started out as a project raising awareness about the situation in one of most beautiful and poorest countries in the world, Nepal. In 2014 I, Nicole, traveld alone to Nepal to volunteer at HORAC (Home of rescue and afflicted children) in Kathmandu, Nepal. I stayed at the orphanage in 5 weeks and learnt a lot of the situation for children in Nepal, their culture and also a lot of my self. In Nepal they have a way of saying - Namasté, my soul honors your soul. Let this photo be the evidence of that your soul honors their souls.

OUR STORY
“We only fight to protect what we love” - David Suzuki

Two newfound friends, united in their fight for a healthy planet and a passion for everything that involves mountains. In a moment of daydreaming on a grey autumn day the seed to an idea was planted. The seed grew to a idea of spending some time in Nepal, a place that was already close to Nicoles heart and a place that had been on Alex mind for a long long time. The idea grew to a plan, that grew into action, and here we are, Alex and Nicole. During the spring of 2017 we will go to Nepal to learn as much as we possibly can, work and gain experience through both social and agricultural projects.

/Nicole, Alexandra and Matilda
